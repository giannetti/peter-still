Boston

Nov. 16, 1853

Dear Sir,

I acknowledge the receipt of a letter from Mr. S. S. Wardwell, of Providence, dated Nov. 15, - enclosing a cheque on the North Bank for 250 dollars: which amt. I have duly placed to your credit:- Say two hundred & fifty dollars.

Yours respectfully,

Ellis Gray Loring.

⟨ [Nov. 18,'53 The above is now credited on Mr. Still's receipt Book.] ⟩

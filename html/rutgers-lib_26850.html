Boston

August 30, 1854

Mr. Peter Still.
Dear sir:

I acknowledge the receipt of a letter from Rev. A. L. Brooks, enclosing a cheque of the Pequonnock Bank of Bridgport, Conn. on the Granite Bank of Boston, for three hundred dollars, dated Aug. 29, 1854, and have placed said amount to your credit, on my books, as directed.

Yours respectfully,

Ellis Gray Loring
by F.L.S.

⟨ When your write again, Mr. Still, I should be interested to know what is the prospect of getting your family out of Slavery; what you now expect to have to pay, &c. Very sincerely, Frederic L. Seymour ⟩

Burlington, N.J.

Oct. 5/52

Dear Cousin John,

Peter Still, who carries this note, is one of the most estimable of men. He wishes to have access to the great hearts of some of the good people of your city, who have great purses.

Please get from him his history, and his object, and direct him what to do. His integrity may be relied on.

Affectionately,

Joseph Parrish

⟨

P.S. You have not been on to see us this year. We should be pleased to have you come. All well. Love to cousin H. & the little ones.

⟩ ⟨ The above letter is from Doctor Joseph Parrish a distinguished physician of New Jersey & well known by his profession in Boston. John P. Robinson Boston Jany, 3. 1853. ⟩
